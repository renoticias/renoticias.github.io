Anker’s handy little 3-port charger is just 35 bucks

Publicado em 29/11/2025 às 23:00

Ver Fonte Original

Earlier this year, I decided to level up my travel game and invest in a charger to bring on trips, rather than raiding my desk outlets every time I packed. I needed a multitasker, something that could power my MacBook Air, charge my phone, and accommodate the random USB-A cord I always seem to need for some reason (I’m looking at you, electric toothbrush). That multitasker turned out to be Anker’s Prime 67W charger, and I haven’t looked back since.

Anker Prime Charger (67W)

The 67W Prime charger includes two USB-C ports and one USB-A along with retractable prongs to make it an easy travel companion.

It’s impressively small considering the power it delivers and the number of ports it offers — two USB-C ports and that single USB-A port. The prongs are retractable, and I welcome every bit of space savings from a travel gadget. It’s usually $49.99, which isn’t terrible considering that it’s basically three chargers in one. At a 30 percent discount, it looks like a mighty powerful little stocking stuffer.
